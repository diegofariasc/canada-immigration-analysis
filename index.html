Welcome to Canada?

A story of opportunity and obstacles

Canada has long stood as a beacon for immigrants worldwide, ranking consistently among the top global destinations for migration. According to the United Nations’ 2022 International Migration Report, Canada welcomed over 450,000 new permanent residents in 2021, reflecting its open immigration policies and economic opportunities.

Research from Statistics Canada (2023) highlights that immigrants contribute significantly to Canada's economic growth, innovation, and demographic sustainability, especially in a context of an aging native-born population. However, successful integration involves navigating complex challenges in housing affordability, labor market access, and healthcare availability.

This narrative visualization aims to shed light on these multifaceted experiences by exploring immigration trends, market pressures, affordability challenges impacting newcomers, unemployment rates and healthcare needs and gaps affecting immigrant communities.
