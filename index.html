Welcome to Canada?

A story of opportunity and obstacles

Canada consistently ranks among the world's top destinations for immigrants, a country many young people (author included) dream of living in. According to the United Nations' 2022 International Migration Report, Canada welcomed over 450k new permanent residents in 2021, reflecting its open immigration policies and economic opportunities.

Research from Statistics Canada (2023) consistently highlights that immigrants contribute significantly to Canada's economic growth, innovation, and demographic sustainability, especially amidst an aging native-born population. However, truly successful integration means navigating complex challenges.

This narrative visualization explores the multifaceted experiences of newcomers. The aim of it is not to discourage, but to provide an honest look at the immigrants' hidden challenges often overlooked by simplified narratives. We will examine immigration trends, market pressures, affordability challenges, persistent unemployment rates, and crucial healthcare needs and gaps. Ultimately, this work urges governments to prioritize not just attracting immigrants, but ensuring their adequate integration too.
